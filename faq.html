Membership

A sunny mart membership is valid for one year from the time application is processed.

New members join online using the website.Note that you will first create an account with an valid email address by selecting 'Register'. You may also required to filled up your personal information.

It may take up to 24 hours for membership to become active when joining or renewing online

You may need to pay RM 10.00 for one year membership of Sunny Mart.

  • As a first-time member, you will receive a 20% discount on your first-time purchase.
  • As a member, you may accumulate member points as RM1 = 1 member point.
  • As a member, you may redeem your member points as RM1 = 100 member points.
  • As a member, you may enjoy various exclusive rewards and benefits from the points that you accumulated from the previos orders.
  • As a member, you may enjoy special discounts/offers during our promotion day that are exclusively offered to a non-member customer.
  • As a member, you may keep track of your order status easily through online.
  • As a member, your personal informations are secured within the system.

Members can cancel their membership within 30 days of joining or renewing for a full return/ refund. After 30 days, no redunf will be issued.
