Our Mart

SUNNY MART is a store that offers both online and physical services. You can find our stores offline, conveniently located near residential areas. So, don't worry about inconvenience when running your grocery errands. Feeling too lazy to step out? No worries! You can simply order at your fingertips.

What can you find in our stores? From fresh vegetables and fruits to cutting-edge electronic devices, we've got it all covered!

Our goal of sustainability aligns perfectly with our mission of 'Grow Together.' ESG (Environmental, Social, and Governance) forms the core concept in managing our stores and establishing our core values. 'E' represents our commitment to the environment. We are an eco-friendly driven company, striving to reduce our carbon footprint by using EVs for deliveries and employing biodegradable packaging for our products. Our mission is in line with international net zero goals.

For the 'S' - the social aspect, we recognize the importance of giving back to society. Therefore, we prioritize employing local individuals in our stores to bolster the local economy.

Lastly, 'G' stands for governance. We maintain transparency in our governance practices and publish annual impact reports on our website. This allows consumers to understand their spending's impact and how we utilize resources for a sustainable future for all.
